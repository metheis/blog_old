Rebuilding My Personal Site

2020 / 07 / 19

I deciding to upgrade my personal website. This includes my art portfolio, dulcimer project site, adding a homepage, and starting this blog. Except for my dulcimer site, which I built on top of a template many years ago, my web pages use a consistent theme across them (a dark navy blue background with light blue text).

I have not yet decided the entire purpose of this blog. I figured it would just be a nice place to post quick updates here and there about things I work on, etc. Especially because documenting my projects may help others. I don't plan to follow any particular schedule or post length requirement. Some posts may be long, while others just a picture, and sometimes I may not post for a while. Hopefully over the course of my lifetime it will simply server as a way for me and others to explore my past.
